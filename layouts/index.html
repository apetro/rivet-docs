Our principles

Rivet is a starting point for UITS applications. Its ready-to-use HTML/CSS components mean that you don’t have to reinvent the wheel for every new project. And its documentation focuses on best practices for accessibility and copy—letting you focus on great user experiences.

We want to make it easier to design and develop great experiences for users across all UITS applications, and you can help. These are our guiding principles to contribute to the design system:

  1. Be clear.

    Make design decisions based on user needs. Design interfaces that help our users accomplish their goals quickly and easily.

  2. Be flexible.

    Our design system is for all types of UITS applications, even ones that have yet to be imagined. We need to design and build for flexibility.

  3. Be productive.

    Done is better than perfect. Iteration is only half the battle. Testing with users could reveal issues or questions we didn’t anticipate.
